David A. Sweeney

PhD Student · Centre for Research into Ecological and Environmental Modelling (University of St Andrews)
Affiliated Researcher · Foundation for Marine Ecology and Telemetry Research (MarEcoTel)

I am a marine mammal scientist and statistical ecologist. My current work involves assessing the short- and long-term impacts of naval training activities on goose-beaked whales in the Southern California Bight using primarily photo-ID, drone, and tag data.

Research interests: marine mammals · impact assessments · biotelemetry · statistical ecology
