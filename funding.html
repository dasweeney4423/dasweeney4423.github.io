Research Funding

List of funding awards for which I have played a significant role in conseptualization, grant writing, and budgeting.

Grants

  • Taking a deeper dive into goose-beaked whale population consequences of disturbance (PCoD)
    • Funder: Office of Naval Research (ONR), BAA N00014-24-S-B001
    • Project span: 2024 – 2029
    • Total project budget: USD $629,508
    • Principal Investigators: Gregory Schorr and Len Thomas
    • Co-investigators: David A. Sweeney, Enrico Pirotta, Erin Falcone, Stephanie Watwood, Stacy DeRuiter, and Gustavo Cárdenas-Hinojosa
    • Lead institution: Foundation for Marine Ecology and Telemetry Research
    • Partner institutions: University of St Andrews; Naval Undersea Warfare Center Division, Newport; Calvin University; Comisión Nacional de Áreas Naturales Protegidas (CONANP), SEMARNAT
    • Project focus: Quantifying population consequences of disturbance (PCoD) for goose-beaked whales by integrating long-term photo-ID, tagging, and drone-based data with advanced statistical and ecological modelling to assess impacts of naval training activities in the Southern California Bight.
